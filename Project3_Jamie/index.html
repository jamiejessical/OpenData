New York City's homeless population presents a multifaceted challenge within the urban landscape. Despite various initiatives, including shelter programs and affordable housing projects, providing adequate support remains a persistent issue.

nyc homeless population

Bowery Residents' Committee

BRC offers shelter, outreach, and other services to homeless individuals in NYC. They can be reached at (212) 803-5700.

New York Cares

This organization offers volunteer opportunities to assist homeless individuals in NYC. While not a helpline, they provide valuable support. Their website is www.newyorkcares.org.

NYC Department of Homeless Services

The DHS operates several programs and shelters for homeless individuals and families in NYC. They can be reached at 311 or through their website.
